Welcome to the VIL Group

We are a research group at Drexel University, focused on advancing computer vision, machine learning, and artificial intelligence to solve fundamental and applied problems in physical 3D human digitization, generative AI, and AI for societal impact.

We always look for passionate students to join the team as RA / externship / internship / visiting students (more info)!

Research Focus

🧍
Physical 3D Human Digitization
Building physics-grounded, biomechanically accurate methods for full-body 3D reconstruction, pose estimation, and motion understanding — enabling a new generation of intelligent human-centric applications. Key projects include BioCoach (AI fitness coaching from video) and BioGait-VLM (gait understanding via vision-language models).
Fitness & Coaching Healthcare Egocentric Vision Humanoid Robotics BioCoach BioGait-VLM
🤖
Generative AI
Building controllable and explainable generative models with applications in healthcare, education, and beyond. We explore geometry-consistent, photorealistic synthesis for humans, scenes, and motion.
Controllable Generation Healthcare Education Scene Synthesis
🌍
Societal Impact
Leveraging AI for healthcare, education, and human-centered applications. We design systems that are fair, inclusive, and deployable in real-world settings — from surveillance understanding to accessibility tools.
